A Modular LIMS from Bharathbrands

BHARATHBRABNDS

In this module, the lab manager shall take the requests for the test about the I.P. & O.P. patients.After the acknowledgement of the samples via the system, the module takes care of the further processes. Two levels of authentication to the values & details of the report via the module maintain the perfection & authenticity of the structure of the report supplied directly to the patients or the consultants.

In addition to the above, the module will also take care of the reagent consumption utilized for the tests. Any change in Critical data affecting the Quality or Finance is audited for administrative control.

Sub-Department Level Browsing Selection

Pending Samples View, Service generated View,Sample Generation, (Sample number generated - In accordance to sample type)Sample Acceptance and lab number generation.Sample Rejection at this end is possible with tagging of cancellation.Screen Report Entry (With Below Given Features) Single Lab number wise test reporting,Parameter-wise test reporting.Test Wise parameter reporting.Patient-wise and order-number-wise test reporting.First Level test report authorization.Second-level test report authorization

Features

Sample Management: Allows labs to enter and manage sample information, including patient data,sample type, and test requests.Workflow Management: Manages the workflow of the laboratory, including sample processing, testing, and analysis.

Instrument Integration

Integrates with laboratory instruments, enabling automatic data transfer and reducing errors.Quality Control: Helps manage quality control measures, including instrument calibration, test validation, and proficiency testing.
