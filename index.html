Who we are

BHARATHBRABNDS

Bharathbrands Limited was established in 2017, a leading software development company in Kenya, India & USA, providing several technological services to promote digital business transformation.

Bharathbrands Pvt Ltd is known for its customer relationship management services concerning software services across the globe. We develop and consult several Application development aspects and technological integrations with clients across the globe.

Bharathbrands is a renowned IT company having two decades of experience in providing solutions to the Healthcare, Banking & Finance and Government Institutions. We are engaged in offering purpose driven feasible custom software solutions and business management solutions services, which include HIMS, LIMS, Medical Drug Distributions System, Patient Care System, Health Information Exchange Platforms, Online Doctors Platform, Clinical Management System for Healthcare Service Providers. We provide Core Banking System, Regulatory Reporting, AML, KYC, LOS, Itax Solutions, Payment Platforms, Mobile Banking and Online Banking Systems for Banking & Finance Institutions. We provide Revenue Management, County Service Requests Management, Platform to Digitize Physical Files in to Electronic Document Management, EKYC systems for Government Institutions.
